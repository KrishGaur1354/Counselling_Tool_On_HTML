WHAT IS COUNSELLING?
Educational Counselling mainly refers to providing assistance and guidance to students in making the right choices in their studies, be it their educational plans, career aspirations, choice of stream and specialisation as well as the selection of college or university as per their interests and preferences.
Required Documents for Counselling:
  • Birth Certificate/10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • AADHAAR CARD
  • Migration Certificate
  • Passport Scan
